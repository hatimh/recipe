I have decided we are going to make Chili! It is not my favourite dish but I like it nevertheless and it is fairly simple to make. You can skip steps by navigating on the menu above.

Brown the Ground Beef in a pan.

Add salt, pepper, onions, chili powder, minced ginger garlic and other seasonings of your choice.

Chop vegtables to the comfort of your mouth! Add them to the spiced meat together with tomato sauce, stewed tomatoes, mushrooms, kidney beans and water. Control amount of water depending on how thick you want your chilli.

Allow to simmer for about 20 mins.

And that's it, Enjoy!.
